About Josh Zhuang

I’m the founder and portfolio manager of The Imperalith Fund. My investment approach blends bottom-up value analysis with a global macro lens. I focus on identifying profitable opportunities across public markets through independent research and fundamental conviction.

I believe long-term capital growth comes from identifying undervalued assets with strong fundamentals. I use a combination of bottom-up research, macroeconomic awareness, and contrarian thinking to uncover opportunities others overlook. Imperalith is not about following the crowd—it’s about conviction, patience, and results.

Our investment theses are often built around asymmetric risk-reward setups and misunderstood narratives. Whether it's tech, industrials, or special situations, I seek companies with durable economics and scalable business models.
