Biography

Josh Zhuang is the Founder and Portfolio Manager of Imperalith Capital Management. He leads the firm’s public markets research, portfolio analysis, and investment communications.

Mr. Zhuang founded Imperalith Capital Management as a research-driven investment platform focused on public equities, valuation, and market strategy. Through the firm, he develops equity research, evaluates public market opportunities, and manages the strategic direction of The Imperalith Fund.

Mr. Zhuang is an Economics student at Boston University with academic interests in financial markets, macroeconomics, valuation, and business strategy. He currently maintains a 4.0 GPA.

Mr. Zhuang now resides in Boston, Massachusetts.
